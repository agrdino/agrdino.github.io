Hungry Buddy Puzzle

Unity Developer — Saigon PNTech

Hungry Buddy Puzzle is a strategic puzzle game where you become a master of queue management and space strategy. Players must organize and manage a queue of hungry “Buddies” — cute animal characters like Elephant, Bird, Hamster, Cat, Rabbit, Dog, Tiger, and Squirrel — helping them get their food in time without causing a jam in the waiting area. The challenge lies in planning moves carefully and expanding or locking slots to handle increasingly complex levels and puzzles.

  • Queue-based puzzle mechanics with deterministic outcomes
  • Easy to add new characters or mechanics via config
  • Scalable for adding boosters or special modes
  • Track level completion, retries, fail points
  • IAP, Ads, Google Firebase, Ads Mediation

Spades Card Game

Unity Developer — FancyCorn Studio

Spades Card Game is a classic trick-taking card game played by four players in teams of two. Players bid on the number of tricks they expect to win, then work with their partner using strategy and timing to meet the bid. Spades are always trump, making every round a mix of planning, teamwork, and smart card play.

  • 2v2 gameplay emphasizing cooperation and coordination
  • Strategic bidding before each round
  • Easy to extend with new rules or modes
  • BOT: Multiple difficulty levels
  • Google Firebase, IAP, Ads Mediation
